NT invests $229m to fix juvenile justice

20:00
Friday, April 20, 2018
Litchfield Room, NT Parliament House, Darwin
20:00 - 20:30
Victor Stojcevski 0477 384 629 Victor.Stojcevski@justice.tas.gov.au
www.eventrelatedurl.com

The Northern Territory Government will spend $229.6 million to implement more than 200 recommendations of the Royal Commission into its broken, scandal-plagued youth justice system.

The Territory’s infamous Don Dale youth detention centre, where leaked video showed youths being tear-gassed and mistreated, and it’s Alice Springs equivalent will be replaced.

The money also includes $66.9 million on a new IT system better linking health and police databases and significant funding for children and families to keep young people out of detention.
